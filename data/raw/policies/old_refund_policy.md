# Acme E-commerce — Refund and Return Policy
**Version:** 2.1  
**Effective Date:** December 1, 2025  
**Owner:** Head of Operations  

---

## Overview

This policy governs all customer refund and return requests for Acme E-commerce. Agents should apply these guidelines consistently across all support channels.

---

## Standard Return Window

Customers may return eligible items within **14 calendar days** of the confirmed delivery date. A valid receipt or order confirmation email is required for all refund requests regardless of order value.

Requests submitted after 14 days will be denied unless escalated to a Tier 2 agent with documented justification.

---

## Eligible Items

Items eligible for refund:

- Physical products in original, unopened packaging
- Defective or damaged items (photographic evidence required within 48 hours of delivery)
- Items significantly different from their product listing description

Items **not** eligible:

- Digital licenses or activated software
- Personalized or custom orders
- Items marked as final sale

---

## Refund Process

1. Customer contacts support with order number and reason for return.
2. Tier 1 agent verifies the request is within the 14-day window.
3. If eligible, agent issues an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number.
4. Customer ships item back within 7 days of RMA issuance. Acme covers return shipping for defective items only.
5. Refund issued within 3–5 business days after item is received and inspected.

---

## Exception Handling

Tier 2 agents may approve extensions of up to 7 additional days (total 21 days) for documented cases. Anything beyond requires VP-level approval.
