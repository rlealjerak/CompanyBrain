## Domestic Shipping (United States)

| Service | Carrier | Estimated Transit | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standard | USPS / FedEx Ground | 5–7 business days | $4.99 or free on orders >$50 |
| Expedited | FedEx Express | 2–3 business days | $12.99 |
| Overnight | FedEx Priority Overnight | Next business day | $29.99 |

Orders placed before 2:00 PM Eastern time ship same day (Monday–Friday). Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends ship the next business day.

## International Shipping

Currently available to Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, France, Australia, and Japan.

| Destination | Estimated Transit | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Canada | 7–10 business days | $14.99 |
| UK / EU | 10–14 business days | $24.99 |
| Australia / Japan | 14–21 business days | $34.99 |

Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the recipient. Acme E-commerce does not collect or reimburse these fees.

Restricted items: batteries, liquids over 100ml, and certain electronics may not be shipped internationally. Verify restrictions in the product catalog before accepting an international order.

## Shipping SLA Breach Policy

A shipping SLA breach occurs when an order arrives more than 5 business days after the guaranteed delivery date as shown in the tracking confirmation email.

On a confirmed SLA breach:
- Tier 1 Support is authorized to issue a full refund of the shipping fee without escalation.
- For orders where the product itself is more than 7 days late, Tier 1 may offer a $15 credit toward a future order.
- For enterprise accounts, any SLA breach must be reported to the assigned Account Manager within 24 hours.

---

## Lost or Damaged Shipments

A shipment is considered lost if tracking shows no update for 5 consecutive business days and the carrier confirms the trace investigation.

For confirmed lost shipments:
- Tier 1 may authorize a replacement shipment for orders under $200.
- Orders $200–$1,000: Tier 2 approval required.
- Orders over $1,000: Operations approval required.

For damaged shipments: the customer must provide photos within 48 hours of delivery. Tier 1 may authorize a replacement or refund per the standard refund policy.

---

## Address Corrections

Customers may update their shipping address before 2:00 PM Eastern on the day the order is placed. After that point, address corrections are not guaranteed and may incur a $5 rerouting fee at the carrier's discretion. Acme is not responsible for packages delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer.
