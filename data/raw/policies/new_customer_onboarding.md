# Acme E-commerce — New Customer Onboarding Checklist
**Version:** 2.0  
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026  
**Owner:** Amy Huang, Customer Success Manager  

---

## Overview

All new enterprise accounts (Growth tier and above) receive a structured 30-day onboarding program. The assigned Account Manager is responsible for completing every item on this checklist. Completion is tracked in Salesforce.

---

## Week 1 — Kickoff and Access

- [ ] Send welcome email within 24 hours of contract signature
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call within 3 business days (45-minute slot)
- [ ] Kickoff call agenda: introductions, goals review, integration overview, Q&A
- [ ] Provision account in the admin portal and confirm admin user has access
- [ ] Introduce customer to their Tier 2 support contact (Marcus Williams or designated agent)
- [ ] Share onboarding resource packet (product guide, API docs link, support portal URL)

---

## Week 2 — Integration and Configuration

- [ ] Follow up on any integration blockers surfaced in Week 1
- [ ] Schedule integration support call if customer has technical integration (30-minute slot with Priya Patel or Engineering on-call)
- [ ] Confirm core product configuration is complete (catalog sync, user provisioning, billing setup)
- [ ] Send mid-onboarding check-in email

---

## Week 3 — Training

- [ ] Schedule training session for the customer's internal team (1-hour slot)
- [ ] Cover: product navigation, reporting, support portal, escalation contacts
- [ ] Share training recording in the customer's Slack channel or via email
- [ ] Confirm at least one non-admin user has logged in and completed at least one transaction

---

## Week 4 — Go-Live Validation

- [ ] 30-day review call (30 minutes)
- [ ] Review: usage metrics, open tickets, any outstanding configuration items
- [ ] Confirm customer knows how to submit refund requests, shipping claims, and billing disputes
- [ ] Transition from onboarding mode to standard account management cadence
- [ ] Log completion in Salesforce and close the onboarding task

---

## Escalation During Onboarding

If a new customer raises a critical issue during the 30-day window:
1. Account Manager handles directly (do not route to Tier 1 during onboarding)
2. Escalate to Sarah Chen if unresolved within 4 hours
3. Log everything in Salesforce with the "onboarding-escalation" tag

A poor onboarding experience is the leading predictor of churn in Year 1. Treat onboarding escalations with VIP-level urgency regardless of account size.

---

## Handoff to Steady State

At the end of Day 30, the Account Manager sends a handoff summary to:
- The customer's primary contact
- Sandra Mitchell (operations log)
- The assigned Tier 2 support agent

The handoff summary includes: completed checklist, known open items, first QBR date (if applicable), and any special handling notes.
