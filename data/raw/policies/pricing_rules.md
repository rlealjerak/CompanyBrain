# Acme E-commerce — Pricing Rules and Discount Authority
**Version:** 1.8  
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026  
**Owner:** Jake Torres, Sales Manager  

---

## Standard Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Seat Limit | Included Support |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $8/seat/month | Up to 10 seats | Email support, 48-hour SLA |
| Growth | $16/seat/month | Up to 50 seats | Email + chat, 24-hour SLA |
| Enterprise | $24/seat/month | Unlimited | Dedicated AM, 1-hour SLA, custom SLAs available |

All prices are per active seat per month, billed annually. Monthly billing is available at a 15% premium.

Annual commitment discount: 10% applied to the per-seat rate before any additional discounts.

---

## Discount Authority

All discounts are applied to the standard per-seat rate (after the annual commitment discount, if applicable).

| Role | Maximum Discount |
|---|---|
| Sales Representative | 15% |
| Sales Manager | 20% |
| VP of Sales or above | 30% |

Discounts exceeding the above thresholds require written approval from the next level up and must be logged in Salesforce before a quote is issued. Verbal approvals are not binding and do not authorize a discounted rate on an invoice.

**Important:** No discount may be applied retroactively to a signed contract without VP of Sales approval and a signed contract amendment.

---

## Enterprise Custom Pricing

For accounts projected to exceed $200K ARR, custom pricing proposals may be submitted to the VP of Sales. These accounts are eligible for:

- Volume-based per-seat rates as low as $18/seat/month (requires VP approval)
- Custom payment terms (standard is Net 30; Net 60 available for accounts >$500K ARR with VP approval)
- Dedicated infrastructure options (quoted separately by Engineering)

## Invoicing and Payment Terms

Standard payment terms are Net 30 from invoice date. Late payments incur 1.5% monthly interest after a 10-day grace period.

For exceptions to payment terms, see the Enterprise Custom Pricing section above. All non-standard terms must appear explicitly in the signed contract.
